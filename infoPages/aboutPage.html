About Us

Here at ElectronicsForCheap.com, when we are faced with an opportunity to offer up great products for a fraction of their cost to millions of customers while at the same time making profit, we take that opportunity and turn it into reality. We are a knock-off startup company based in San Francisco Bay Area. We had the genius idea of starting this online business with the inspiration we got from our professor for CIS 317 Section 03 class, Professor Bierman. We first started off by contacting shady suppliers from Guangdou who promised to make quality parts for our company for the cheapest price. But we were wrong. Instead we got ripped off and learned our first lesson. Never do business with an unreputable seller. After getting scammed 5 more times, we finally landed on a solid supplier who would be supplying the very products you see listed on our page today for the right cost. We are delighted that you are on this journey with us. Thank you for your generous contributions.
