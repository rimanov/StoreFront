Popular questions


How long should I expect to wait for my item?

We ship within the United States and internationally. So expect maximum wait time of 1 week - 5 weeks for your order to arrive



If I dont like the item I received or it is damaged, what do I do?

We provide free returns if the issue was not caused directly by the buyer. see Our Return Policy



Are the items you sell authentic?

Absolutely NOT. The items we sell are knock-offs. So buy at your own risk



What are the payment methods currently accepted?

At the moment, we only accept Credit/Debit cards and Paypal payments.



If I don't have a PayPal account, can I still make payment with PayPal?

Yes! It is commonly misunderstood that a Paypal account is needed in order to make payments through Paypal. The truth is you DO NOT need one, although we strongly recommend you sign up to enjoy the added ease of use. Without a Paypal account, all you need is any Debit/Credit card stated below that is supported by Paypal. By using Paypal, we can process & deliver your orders to you in a shorter time. Paypal is the easiest & most secure way to make payment online. No account needed.



I am having trouble accessing the store and pages load in a weird way. Am I using the right browser?

As ElectronicsForCheap uses some of the latest graphics designs which may not be supported in lower version of browsers, it is recommended that you use the following browsers to access: 1. Google Chrome. 2. Mozilla FireFox.



How much is the Handling Fee? Are there any other fees?

The flat-rate handling fee is $5.99. Handling fee covers the delivery, material, labour and logistics cost to support the sampling service. This is the only fee we currently charge



Do I have to pay customs fees if I live in a different country?

We send all items at very low declared values beneath the customs thresholds for the countries concerned. Most countries we send customs declared value as less than $10.



Do the Airpod Pros have the H1 or W1 Chip the same as the original ones?

Yes our models have the H1 or W1 chips.



Do you have a guarantee? What if I have a problem with my item?

. We provide 30 Days from date of delivery guarantee against major faults or defects. Our Refund Policy can be clearly viewed here and is binding on all purchases.



My order is lost. What do I do? Am I covered by ElectronicsForCheap?

If your shipment has been lost and you did not choose shipping insurance then we cannot do anything but refund your order minus the cost of the item we lost.



How can I track my order? Will I be sent an email confirmation?

Sometimes customers ask "Where Is My Order?" or "How Can I Track My Order?" and with ElectronicsForCheap that is very easy to do. Every time a customer orders on our website they are automatically provided tracking information that is sent to their email address they used to purchase with.



If I made the order by mistake, can I cancel my order and not get charged?

If your item is in Processing, you can cancel it online before it ships. Go to the checkout page and select an item to see your order details, then select Cancel Item.



Will I be charged tax on my order? If so, how is the amount determined

In accordance with state and local law, your purchases from ElectronicsForCheap will be taxed using the applicable sales tax or seller's use tax rate for your shipping address.
