How to return an item


Gather the purchases you want to return, along with the original contents and packaging

If you received a packing slip, place the return form portion of it in the box with your return. If you did not receive a packing slip, you may get a pre-populated return form upon approval. For approval, please Contact Us with your Order ID and we will be glad to help. Then, place the completed form in the box with your return. Put the return label on the package, add postage and mail it via the carrier of your choice.
